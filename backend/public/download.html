Get started in 3 steps

1

Install the app

Download the installer for your OS and run it. On macOS, drag to Applications. On Windows, run the .exe.

2

Create your account

Open the app and create an account with your email. Your 14-day free trial starts immediately — no credit card needed.

3

Start organizing

Choose a folder for your data, and start capturing tasks in markdown. The app generates daily files, syncs tasks across projects, and keeps everything in plain .md files you own.
