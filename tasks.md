Add tasks here and they'll sync to your project files when you save.

**Format:** `- [ ] Task name | due date | project`

- Each line starts with `- [ ]` (that's dash, space, open bracket, space, close bracket)
- Then your task name
- Then `|` and a due date like `2026-02-15` (optional)
